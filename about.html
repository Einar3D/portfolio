About me

Greetings,

Thank you for visiting my humble website. I created these webpages as an exercise to learn about front-end web development with JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. Right now, it's just a way for me to continue learning about 3D modeling which is pretty fun. I would like to create some high quality content in the near future.

In terms of 3D art, I have basic knowledge of

  • Blender
  • Substance Painter
  • GIMP
  • Unreal Engine

Creating 3D environments in Unreal Engine, with assets from Blender, is a process I am familiar with. Further, I have practical experience with setting up artificial intelligence via behavior trees in Unreal Engine 4, for 3rd person combat scenarios, similar to Dark Souls III.

An entry level 3D artist position, where I could provide valuable assistance to a team and learn on the job would be an appealing opportunity. As an international applicant, I would also be able to work at a very reasonable salary. Lastly, I am a dual citizen, so I would be able to work in the USA.
